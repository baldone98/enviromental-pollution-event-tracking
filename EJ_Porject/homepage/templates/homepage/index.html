Welcome to Our Environmental Justice Site

This site is dedicated to uncovering and discussing issues of environmental injustice in Tennessee. We provide insights and news updates on how environmental decisions impact different communities across the state, particularly focusing on how these decisions affect marginalized and disadvantaged groups.

Our goal is to bring awareness to these critical issues and foster a community that can advocate for fair and equitable environmental practices. Here, you can find articles, analysis, and resources that help illuminate the challenges and actions being taken to address environmental injustice.
